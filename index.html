Lionel Messi

The Greatest of All Time

Lionel Messi is seen as one the greatest soccer players of all time. He was born on June 24, 1987 in Rosario, Argentina. He started playing soccer at a young age and then was scouted by FC Barcelona's famous La Masia academy and went on to show the world what is possible on the soccer field.

He spent most of his time at FC Barcelona, where he scored 672 goals and won 10 La Liga titles and 4 UEFA Champions League trophies. He later went on to play for Paris Saint-Germain before moving to Inter Miami CF. In 2022, he finally claimed the one trophy that was missing, the FIFA World Cup, he led Argentina to victory in Qatar.
